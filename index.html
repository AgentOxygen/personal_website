Hello there! My name is Cameron,


I am a senior undergrad at the University of Texas at Austin majoring in computational engineering and a research assistant in the Persad Aero-Climate Group. I use gridded climate simulation data to study the effects of anthropogenic aerosols on various facets of climate change. I specialize in the computational side of climate science research, developing coding solutions and full-stack applications. I am currently writing a research paper that analyzes the effects of aerosols on patterns of heatwave risk and developing a web application to visualize model agreement on the hydroclimate of California.
